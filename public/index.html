About me

Hello, my name is The My. I’m working as a web designer & front-end developer based in HCM City, Vietnam.

For the last 5 years I’ve worked with clients to provide responsive web design, front-end development, and WordPress integrations.

I embrace creativity in all aspects of life. When I am not pushing pixels, I enjoy cooking, painting, gardening, and playing the ukelele. I love to travel and I'm deeply inspired by the diversity of cultures and the natural beauty of the Earth.
